Put Your Medicare Claims Information to Work For You

MyMedicare.gov's Blue Button provides you an easy way to put your data to work for you in the devices and applications you trust. Switching on the advanced functionality of Medicare's next generation BlueButton service lets you setup unique passwords for each device you use and lets you remove or change the password if you lose a device. 

To use this feature you will setup a device profile and copy the unique credentials (a custom user name and password). You can save those credentials and use them whenever you connect to the BlueButton service from that device.
