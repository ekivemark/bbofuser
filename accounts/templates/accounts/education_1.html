It's Your Data

Taking Your Data with you

When you switch on BlueButton 2.0 you are giving MyMedicare.gov permission to  send your data to a third party application.  

Once your data leaves MyMedicare.gov and is sent to an application you have chosen we no longer control that information. You do. Before you connect an application to your MyMedicare information make sure that you trust that applicztion to take good care of your information. 

Don't worry. If you change your mind you can come back to your account dashboard on MyMedicare.gov and disconnect the applications you want to stop using. Your dashboard also gives you the option to control how long an application has access to your information. When that time limit is reached we will ask you if you want to extend the time.
