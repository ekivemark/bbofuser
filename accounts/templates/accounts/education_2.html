So you want to switch on BlueButton 2.0

Here are the next steps:

1. We will ask you to "sign" a form confirming that you want to be able to send your health information to applications of YOUR choosing.
2. We will ask you a series of questions to help us confirm that you are the person asking for your data.
3. We will ask you to update your profile with phone information. This will let us offer you the option to use a pin code as an extra layer of security when connecting an application to your MyMedicare information.

We will send you a message to your registered email address to confirm each step that you complete.

The final email message in the sign up process will have a link that you must click on to activate BlueButton 2.0 on your account. 

As a security safeguard we will also send a message to you whenever an application is approved to access your MyMedicare.gov record.
