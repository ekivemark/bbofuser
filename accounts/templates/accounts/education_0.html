A New Generation of BlueButton

Put your health information to work for you

MyMedicare.gov introduces a new and advanced BlueButton service. The new features allow you to connect your Medicare information to third party applications that you have chosen to trust.

BlueButton 2.0 lets you choose where you want to save and manage your health information. We then make it easy for you to connect your data and keep it up to date.
