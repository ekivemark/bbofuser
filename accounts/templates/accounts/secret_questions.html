Keep your Information Safe

Medicare takes the protection of your health information very seriously.

Help us to protect your information by providing us with information to protect your account:

- Secret  Questions and Answers
- Your mobile phone number (if you have one)

We will use your secret questions and their answers to challenge you when you connect a new Sub-account to BlueButton 2.0.

If you provide a mobile phone number and the cellphone carrier name you can choose to have a pin code sent to your cellphone when you connect to the BlueButton 2.0 service.
